# Inference Grid

## Style Prompt
A cinematic systems portfolio for an AI engineer whose work lives at the intersection of LLMs, data infrastructure, and product execution. The visual language should feel analytical and high-confidence, but never sterile: luminous signal paths, disciplined typography, dark control-room surfaces, and a sense of motion that suggests ideas moving through systems. The portfolio should feel like a live intelligence surface rather than a static resume.

## Colors
- Base Ink: #06101b
- Deep Slate: #102236
- Cold Mist: #edf3fb
- Signal Cyan: #65e7ff
- Vector Lime: #d4ff3f

## Typography
- Headlines and interface text: Space Grotesk
- Metrics, dates, and technical labels: IBM Plex Mono

## Motion Rules
- Entrances snap into place with confidence, then settle.
- Ambient background elements drift slowly to keep every frame alive.
- Scene changes use angled panel wipes, not crossfades.
- Metrics should feel earned: count up, fill, or trace into view.

## What Not To Do
- No purple-heavy cyberpunk gradients.
- No glassmorphism, frosted cards, or generic SaaS hero layouts.
- No empty centered title-on-background compositions.
- No fake browser chrome, dashboard screenshots, or stock UI boxes.
- No vague copy like "innovative solutions" without proof points.
