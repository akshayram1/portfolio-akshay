# Storyboard

## Format
- Canvas: 1920x1080
- Duration: 26 seconds
- Style: Inference Grid

## Scene 1 | 0.0 - 4.3
- Goal: Establish identity immediately.
- Layout: Large two-line name on the left, role and summary on the right, proof chips at the bottom.
- Motion: Signal nodes fade in, name rises in with stagger, summary card slides in from the right.
- Transition: Cyan diagonal wipe.

## Scene 2 | 3.8 - 8.4
- Goal: Turn the resume into evidence.
- Layout: Oversized lead metric with three support bars and labels.
- Motion: Count-up numerics and fill bars.
- Transition: Lime diagonal wipe.

## Scene 3 | 7.8 - 12.6
- Goal: Show career progression and platform work.
- Layout: Vertical timeline spine with role cards and impact notes.
- Motion: Timeline draws, role cards stack in with depth.
- Transition: Cyan diagonal wipe.

## Scene 4 | 12.0 - 17.2
- Goal: Spotlight flagship project work.
- Layout: Dominant project panel on the left, secondary award-winning project on the right, connected by system traces.
- Motion: Diagram traces animate in, chips rise into place.
- Transition: Lime diagonal wipe.

## Scene 5 | 16.9 - 21.5
- Goal: Build trust with awards, research, and certifications.
- Layout: Three trust columns plus a horizontal tech ribbon.
- Motion: Column headers punch in, ribbon scrolls subtly.
- Transition: Cyan diagonal wipe.

## Scene 6 | 21.0 - 26.0
- Goal: Land the positioning and contact.
- Layout: Big closing statement with contact rail and availability note.
- Motion: Statement enters in tiers, final scene fades down at the end.
